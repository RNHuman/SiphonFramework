Intro

The world of industrial piping is vast, dense and overflowing with information. We aim to make the task of creating and thriving in this industry not only easier, but INSPIRING.

Grow Your business with us. Produce drawings and job cards in seconds to keep your workshop and tenders running smooth and fast.

Any catalogue pipe you need, Siphon Framework Piping Library can make it; with over 70 pipe variations, 5000+ flange configurations per scene and 100K+ drawings, the power is in your hands.

The Siphon Framework Piping Library covers the entire industrial piping catalogue; Spools, Pulled Bends, Sweep Tees and beyond. Even a Duck Foot here and there.

The Siphon Framework goal is simple: to make your business better, your drawings beautiful, and your workflow optimal.
